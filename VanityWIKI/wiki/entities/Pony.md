# Pony (Recurring Character)

## Overview
**Pony** is a recurring character appearing in osyra42's creative works, consistently associated with horses, friendship, and pivotal plot events involving magical artifacts.

## Appearances by Work

### Ever Diamond (2007)
**Role:** Co-protagonist, recipient of Ever Diamond

**Characteristics:**
- 16 years old
- Tom's best friend
- Horse enthusiast, owns horse named Firestar (colt named Firewing)
- Land Fighter
- Wears hoodie
- Receives protective ring from Tom
- Given Ever Diamond by Tom via Brittany

**Key Events:**
- Shot in head by Matthew
- Saved by combination of guardian angel ring and Ever Diamond
- Soul becomes tied to Ever Diamond (visible as blood-red inside ring)
- Must kill Matthew to restore soul (according to Lela)
- Hospitalized but heals "incredibly fast"
- Receives Angel's Tear Drop sapphire

**Relationships:**
- **Tom**: Best friend, romantic interest
- **Brittany**: Friend, delivers Ever Diamond
- **Cassie**: Friend, protects her
- **Teagan**: Friend, fellow fighter
- **Lela**: Knows from year ago, Lela gave her horse
- **Kelsey**: Caretaker at hospital

### Infinite Devastation (2011)
**Role:** Taylor's best friend, guide to Gray Area

**Characteristics:**
- Taylor's best friend
- Owns Clydesdale mare named Millie
- Knows about Lela and Gray Area from year ago
- Has copper chain necklace for magic protection
- Died in school shootings one year prior
- Saved by Lela's attempt to use Time Shard
- Can enter Gray Area using special key

**Key Events:**
- Reveals magical world to Taylor
- Explains Ever Diamond history
- Shows Taylor the Gray Area
- Fights alongside Taylor and Lela
- Battles Waste Makers
- Loses sword when enemy tramples it
- Reunited with Tommy (Tom) from captivity

**Relationships:**
- **Taylor**: Best friend, protects and guides
- **Lela**: Friend from year ago, saved her life
- **Tommy (Tom)**: Knows from captivity, reunited
- **Chris Anders**: Young boy they rescue

## Archetype Analysis
Pony represents:
- **The Innocent**: Pure-hearted, often victimized
- **The Protected**: Others sacrifice for her safety
- **The Connection**: Links protagonists to magical elements
- **The Survivor**: Survives seemingly fatal events
- **The Horse Person**: Consistently associated with horses

## Character Development
- **Ever Diamond**: Passive recipient of help, protected by others
- **Infinite Devastation**: Active protector, guides Taylor, fighter
- Shows growth from protected to protector across works

## Connections
- **Horses**: Always has horse companion (Firestar/Firewing, Millie)
- **Tom/Tommy**: Connected to male character named Tom
- **Lela**: Relationship with goddess/mentor figure
- **Magical Artifacts**: Central to artifact plots (Ever Diamond, Time Shard)
- **Near-Death Experience**: Shot in Ever Diamond, died in Infinite Devastation

## Theories
1. **Soul Fragment Theory**: Different versions are fragments of same soul
2. **Reincarnation**: Same soul reborn in different worlds
3. **Archetype Manifestation**: Author's embodiment of "protected friend" archetype
4. **Timeline Variations**: Different outcomes of same person in alternate timelines
