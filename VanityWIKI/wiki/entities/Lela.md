# Lela (Recurring Character)

## Overview
**Lela** is a recurring character name appearing across multiple works by osyra42, taking different forms in each universe but often associated with magic, immortality, and powerful artifacts.

## Appearances by Work

### Ever Diamond (2007)
**Role:** Ancient Weapons Master, rogue turned ally

**Characteristics:**
- Former Elder of a village
- Possesses Cosmin (magical weapon)
- Sold the Ever Diamond to Cassie
- Knows the history of magical artifacts
- Appears as goddess-like figure
- Helps the protagonists understand the Ever Diamond's power

**Key Actions:**
- Recovered Ever Diamond from king's tomb 3 years before story
- Sold it to Cassie (jeweler)
- Reveals ring's true nature and history
- Helps group understand Pony's condition after shooting

### Infinite Devastation (2011)
**Role:** Goddess trapped in Gray Area

**Characteristics:**
- Immortal goddess, not affected by time
- Long silky bright red hair with white ribbon
- Pale skin, wears dress with purple flowers
- Trapped in surreal "Gray Area" world
- Cut palm bleeds, binding Taylor to her through blood
- Former village Elder
- Gave Ever Diamond to Pony in connected story

**Powers:**
- Can move between worlds
- Teaches magic (Life Spirit, summoning)
- Immortal and timeless
- Can teleport others
- Summons weapons with shimmer of light

**Key Actions:**
- Bound Taylor to help her escape Gray Area
- Reveals Pony died in school shootings, tried to save her with Time Shard
- Time Shard blast shattered Ever Diamond and killed Lela (but she's immortal)
- Trains Taylor in staff combat and magic
- Leads group against Waste Makers

- **The Mentor**: Guides protagonists through magical mysteries

- **The Connected One**: Links different characters to larger magical systems
- **The Sacrificed**: Often suffers for power or knowledge

- **Pony**: Connected to Pony in both works (as friend in one, as goddess in other)
