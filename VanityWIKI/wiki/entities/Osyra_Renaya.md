# Osyra Renaya (Operation Chimera)

## Overview
**Dr. Osyra Renaya** is the protagonist of Operation Chimera, a brilliant geneticist who leads the Chimera Project to splice animal DNA into human subjects. Her journey takes her from ambitious researcher to morally compromised creator to isolated hunter "Chimera One" with wolf-like traits.

## Identity
- **Full Name**: Dr. Osyra Renaya
- **Title**: Operator-Level Researcher (SCP Foundation)
- **Call Sign**: Chimera One
- **Clearance**: Level 3, Site 17, Genetics Division
- **Species**: Human (Chimera-enhanced, wolf traits)
- **Status**: Active mercenary asset, Foundation-affiliated

## Physical Description

### Pre-Transformation
- **Height**: Tall (mentioned as "tall for age/gender")
- **Build**: Awkward, repressed
- **Hair**: Black, often falling into face
- **Eyes**: Brown (pre-transformation)
- **Skin**: Light brown
- **Age**: College-age in early chapters, older in Ch. 90

### Post-Transformation (Ch. 90)
- **Eyes**: Red, "striking and unsettling"
- **Ears**: Wolf ears (dark fur, erect, alert)
- **Tail**: Bushy grey wolf's tail
- **Hair**: Long black, worn in messy ponytail
- **Skin**: Brown, smudged with dirt
- **Abilities**: Superhuman speed, strength, enhanced senses

## Personality Traits

### Positive
- Brilliant scientist (viral vector research)
- Strategic thinker
- Determined, focused
- Capable leader (recruits and manages team)
- Self-aware (recognizes her moral failings)

### Negative
- Morally flexible (bypasses ethics)
- Manipulative (tricks Layla)
- Ambitious to a fault ("god complex")
- Desensitized to suffering
- Self-isolating

### Evolution
- **Early**: Isolated, seeking validation through achievement
- **Recruitment**: Tempted by unlimited resources
- **Mid**: Crossing ethical lines, desensitized
- **Late**: Guilt-ridden, recognizes predation
- **Final**: Isolated hunter, living between worlds
