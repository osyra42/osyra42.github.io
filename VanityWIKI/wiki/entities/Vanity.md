# Vanity (Autonomous Construct)

## Overview
**Vanity** is a highly advanced autonomous android construct housed in a humanoid chassis, originally created by Layla McKay's parents through their tech company. She serves as Osyra Renaya's only constant companion, monitor, and silent judge. Her true programming and purpose remain partially mysterious, with evidence of foreign "organic" code integrated into her systems.

- **Designation**: Autonomous Construct Vanity (V-73)
- **Classification**: Pending (SCP Foundation)

- **Call Sign**: Unit V-73
