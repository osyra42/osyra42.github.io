# Layla McKay (Chimera Subject)

## Overview
**Layla McKay** is the younger sister of Nala McKay, daughter of deceased tech entrepreneurs, and the first successful human subject of the Chimera Project. After inheriting her parents' estate and discovering their connection to Site 17, she is kidnapped by Osyra Renaya and transformed into a Chimeran with wolf-like traits.
