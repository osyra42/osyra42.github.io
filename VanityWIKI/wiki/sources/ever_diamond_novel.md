# Ever Diamond - Fantasy Adventure Novel

## Overview
**Ever Diamond** is a fantasy adventure novel written by osyra42 in 2007. The story follows a group of young protagonists who discover magical artifacts and become embroiled in a quest involving ancient powers, murder, and redemption.

## Publication Details
- **Written:** 2007
- **Author:** Coffee Byte Dev (osyra42)
- **Genre:** Fantasy Adventure
- **Status:** Complete (archived)

## Plot Summary

### Part I: Ever Diamond
The story begins with Tom, a 16-year-old who feels different from others. He gives Pony a protective ring and asks Brittany to deliver the Ever Diamond to her. The Ever Diamond is revealed to be an ancient, indestructible 4-inch diamond ring with time-viewing and teleportation abilities.

Pony is shot by Matthew, who wants the Ever Diamond. The ring (combined with a guardian angel ring) saves her life, but her soul is now tied to the ring, visible as blood-red coloring inside the diamond.

### Key Magical Elements

#### The Ever Diamond
- 4-inch ring made of pure diamond
- Indestructible
- Can see past, present, and future
- Teleportation capability
- Contains Pony's soul after she's shot
- History: Made by Protectorates for a King, hidden in tomb, recovered by Lela 3 years ago

#### Black Oak Box
- Heavy, well-crafted container for Ever Diamond
- Made with lead in bottom
- Originally meant to be thrown in sea to be forgotten

#### Angel's Tear Drop
- Blue tear-shaped sapphire
- Given to Pony while hospitalized
- Chain can be added via Cassie's keyblade

#### Keyblade
- 4-inch key that extends to 4 feet with sharp blade
- Glows with mystical light
- Used by Cassie

#### Holy Time Charm
- Allows going back in time to change one thing
- Not affected by Ever Diamond
- Hidden in Eastern Lands
- Tom's quest item to save Pony

### Story Arc
1. Tom gives Pony protective ring
2. Pony receives Ever Diamond from Brittany
3. Matthew shoots Pony, steals box (but not ring)
4. Pony's soul tied to Ever Diamond (turns blood red)
5. Cassie catches Matthew
6. Group learns Pony must kill Matthew to restore soul
7. Tom goes on quest with friends to find Holy Time Charm
8. Teleported to fantasy world (Blackshire village)
9. Equipped with armor and weapons
10. Meet Jaimee, Buck, and others
11. Quest to save Pony continues
