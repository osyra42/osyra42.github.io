# Chapter 15 - The Warehouse

## Overview
Layla inherits her parents' tech warehouse and discovers it houses the secret to Vanity's missing security chip, setting her on a path to complete her parents' unfinished work.

## Key Events

### Inheritance and Setup
- **Layla turns 18**: Inherits parents' estate
- **Sister Nala**: Returned briefly for legalities, then resumed missionary work abroad
- **Family home sold**: To settle taxes and debts
- **Layla's guardian**: Nancy (steadfast guardian and confidante)
- **Layla's condition**: Growth hormone deficiency (4'11" stature, non-life-threatening)

### The Warehouse
- **Status**: One of last remaining assets of parents' tech empire
- **Operations**: Barely operational, ~30 employees
- **Product**: Home assistant androids (basic, outdated models)
- **Comparison**: Vanity is "decades ahead" in technology
- **Layla's visit**: First time seeing the facility, compelled to assess as legal owner

### Arrival Scene
- **Security challenge**: Armed guards (5 total, led by scarred man)
- **Vanity's intervention**: Steps out to handle situation
- **Verification**: Layla provides inheritance paperwork
- **Tension**: Guards initially hostile, become cautiously respectful
- **Vanity's role**: Protector and representative

### Facility Tour
- **Manager**: Daniel Harris (cautious but welcoming)
- **Scale**: Massive facility with assembly lines
- **Production**: Sleek modern androids, but inferior to Vanity
- **Emotional impact**: Layla feels pride in parents' legacy
- **Vanity's presence**: Stands out as advanced design

### The Discovery
- **Layla's question**: Asks about Vanity's blueprints
- **Daniel's revelation**: Locked study room, unused for years
- **Nala's envelope**: Left for Layla with brass key and letter
- **Letter contents**: 
  - Parents wanted Layla to have what's in study
  - "Trust Vanity. She's more than she seems."
  - Encouragement to be careful

### The Study Room
- **Contents**: Old files, blueprints, prototypes
- **Key find**: "Project Vanity" blueprints
- **Critical discovery**: Missing security chip documentation
- **Chip function**: Critical for multitasking and operational integrity
- **Absence implication**: Significant vulnerability in Vanity's design

### The Mystery Deepens
- **Layla's questions**:
  - Why don't you have the chip?
  - Why didn't parents install it?
  - Where is the chip now?
- **Vanity's theories**:
  - Parents unable to acquire/manufacture chip
  - Intentionally left out as safeguard
  - Ethical concerns about full capability
- **Facility records**: Show canceled order from specialized manufacturer
- **Possibilities**: Chip never produced, or made off-the-books

### Resolution and Commitment
- **Layla's decision**: Must find the chip, finish parents' work
- **Vanity's pledge**: "I will help you, Layla. But we must be careful."
- **Warning**: Chip could "change everything"
- **Guard presence**: Unnamed guard watches them leave
- **Daniel's task**: Asked to investigate chip's existence/location
- **Drive home**: Quiet contemplation, determination

## Characters

### Layla McKay
- **Age**: 18 (recently inherited)
- **Physical**: 4'11" (growth hormone deficiency), appears younger
- **Personality**: Determined, respectful of legacy, anxious but brave
- **Role**: Legal owner of warehouse, heir to tech empire
- **Motivation**: Honor parents' memory, complete their work
- **Development**: From anxious heir to determined investigator

### Vanity (in this chapter)
- **Appearance**: Violet eyes, calm demeanor, 4'11" (similar to Layla)
- **Relationship**: Lives with Layla and Nancy, "almost mother and child"
- **Capability**: Advanced beyond facility's current production
- **Mystery**: Missing security chip, undocumented capabilities
- **Role**: Protector, assistant, key to parents' legacy
- **Development**: Revealed as more than assistant - central to mystery

### Nancy
- **Role**: Layla's guardian and confidante
- **Status**: Welcomed Vanity into home
- **Significance**: Steady adult presence in Layla's life
- **Connection**: Knew Layla's parents, trusted by family

### Nala McKay
- **Relation**: Layla's older sister
- **Occupation**: Missionary work abroad
- **Action**: Handled legal transfer, left envelope for Layla
- **Message**: "Trust Vanity. She's more than she seems."
- **Mystery**: What does she know about parents' work?

### Daniel Harris
- **Role**: Facility manager
- **Attitude**: Cautious but cooperative
- **Knowledge**: Aware of facility's struggles, respectful of legacy
- **Task**: Investigate missing chip

### The Guards
- **Initial stance**: Hostile, armed, suspicious
- **Leader**: Scarred man, protocol-focused
- **Shift**: Become cautiously respectful after verification
- **Function**: Represent tension between old and new ownership
