## Commission Structure

### Paid Commissions
- **Starting Price**: $20
- **Payment**: Via donate page, no refunds once work begins
- **Timeline**: ~3 months average, depending on complexity and queue
- **Policy**: One active commission per person
- **Scope Changes**: May reset queue position
- **Right to Decline**: May decline any request for any reason

### Free Commissions
- Available with active communication throughout project
- Projects dropped if client ghosts
