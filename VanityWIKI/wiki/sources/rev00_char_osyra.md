# Character Dossier: Osyra (Revision 00)

## Overview
Character dossier for Osyra, protagonist of Operation Chimera Revision 00, covering her from late teens to altered adulthood.

## Key Claims, Facts, and Dates
- **Base Appearance (Pre-Accident):** Light brown skin, black hair often falling into her face, brown eyes, tall for her age/gender with awkward build
- **Altered Appearance (Post-Lab Accident):** Ominous red eyes that glow faintly in low light; overall physiology fundamentally altered, making her appear monstrous
- **Psychological Profile:** Intellectually gifted, fiercely independent, socially alienated, repressed rage, prideful to a fault, latent protective instinct
- **Core Conflict:** Need for genuine acknowledgement/connection vs. pride, intellectual arrogance, shattered sense of self
- **Background:** Extreme wealth (massive estate/mansion), neglectful parents, Derran the Butler as closest parental figure
- **Lab Accident:** Unintended, catastrophic event that mutated her body and severed her from old life
- **Motivations:** Teens - earn parents' love through achievement; Adult - grapple with trauma, answer "Vanity... am I a monster?"
- **Skills:** Academic/scientific prowess (especially Biology), violent capability when protective rage triggered, resilience
