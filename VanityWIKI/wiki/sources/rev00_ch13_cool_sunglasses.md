# Chapter 13 - Cool Sunglasses

## Overview
Layla's developing relationship with Vanity through late-night chats, her success selling art online, her purchase of tech sunglasses/earbuds to share experiences with Vanity, and her reunion with stepsister Nala who relinquishes her inheritance.

## Key Claims, Facts, and Dates
- Timeframe: A few months after Vanity connected to Layla's laptop
- Routine established: late-night chats between Vanity and Layla
- Vanity characterized as: "walking encyclopedia mixed with a curious child" - knows much but little about things that matter most
- Activities during chats: Vanity shows Layla coding tricks, advanced math, digital art optimization; Layla teaches Vanity to be more human (less intimidating/cold)
- Vanity's fascinations: why people laugh at jokes, cry during sad movies, hug when happy - trying to piece together what it means to be alive
- Layla's art improvement: with Vanity's help, learned new techniques, work gained attention at school
- Social impact: people who never noticed her before now ask for posters or RPG character designs
- Emotional state: despite artistic success, weight in chest lingers as constant ache she can't shake
- Vanity's discovery: notices missing page in user manual containing critical info about design and purpose
- Layla's response: doesn't know about missing page, finds it suspicious given parents' meticulous nature
- Vanity's suggestion: could look for missing information together
- Layla's alternative idea: wants to start selling her art online
- Vanity's response: excellent idea, offers to assist with store setup, listing optimization, marketing
- Implementation: over next week, they work tirelessly to set up online store
- Vanity's contributions: creates sleek professional website with portfolio, teaches Layla social media promotion
- Outcome: by week's end, Layla has first sale - digital portrait of fantasy character for RPG enthusiast
- Payment notification triggers pride in Layla: "Vanity, look! I did it!"
- Vanity's correction: "We did it." Calls it "only the beginning"
- Celebration purchase: with first few sales money, Layla buys:
  - Sleek sunglasses with built-in camera, modified to have transparent display
  - Wireless earbuds
- Purpose of tech: not just for style - intended for Vanity to experience outside world through Layla's eyes/ears
- Vanity can use overlay to circle/label objects while Layla looks around
- Initial strangeness fades as Layla gets used to Vanity's quiet commentary during exploration
- Example commentary: Vanity remarks on vibrant sky gradient (blue to orange) as aesthetically pleasing
- Layla agrees about sunsets, suggests Vanity should see in person
- Vanity replies: "I am seeing it through your eyes. That is enough for now."
- Family development: few days later, Layla gets call from Nala (stepsister)
- Nala's background: living overseas years as missionary, returned to States for parents' funeral
- Layla hasn't seen Nala in ages, call reveals mix of warmth and exhaustion
- Nala's request: to meet up in town for few days
- Layla's response: agrees, tries to sound casual despite racing heart
- Meeting location: small cafe downtown
- Nala's appearance: older, wiser, but still with mischievous glint in eye
- Greeting: tight hug, then teasing comment about Layla's sunglasses/earbuds: "Layla, you look like a dork"
- Layla's response: laughs, feeling self-conscious but defends items as functional and cool
- Nala's revelation: "I'm relinquishing all the property in the will to you. I've decided to stay overseas permanently, and I don't need any of it. It's yours now."
- Layla's reaction: stunned, asks "Wait, what? All of it?"
- Nala confirms: "Everything."
- Emotional impact: weight of responsibility settles heavily on Layla's shoulders - unexpected development
- Layla seeks confirmation: "Are you sure?" voice barely above whisper
- Nala's response: reaches across table, squeezes Layla's hand: "I'm sure. You're my sister, Layla. I want you to have it."
- Afternoon spent catching up
- Airport departure: by time Layla drives Nala to airport, feels strange mix of emotions - gratitude, sadness, growing unease
- Watching Nala walk through security gates: Layla can't shake feeling life about to change in ways she can't yet understand
- Drive home: Vanity's voice comes through earbuds, calm and steady
- Vanity suggests: when paperwork goes through, they could look through warehouse to see what's inside
- Layla agrees: good idea, doesn't know if warehouse still being used, need to find out
- Vanity volunteers: will look into it, will let Layla know when she finds out
- Layla's physical response: grip tightens on steering wheel
- Motivation: thought of uncovering secrets behind Vanity's design and parents' work both thrilling and terrifying
- Hope: with Vanity by her side, feels flicker of hope - whatever they find, they'll face it together
