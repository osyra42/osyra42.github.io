# Content Warning for Operation Chimera

## Overview
This document contains content warnings for the *Operation Chimera* series, detailing mature and potentially disturbing material present across all revisions.

## Key Claims, Facts, and Dates
- *Operation Chimera* contains mature content including mental health themes, sexual content, medical experimentation, violence, cosmic horror, and noir tones
- Specific warnings include:
  - Mental Health & Trauma: self-harm, suicidal ideation, childhood neglect and abuse, psychological distress, identity crises, substance use
  - Sexual Content: mature sexual themes and suggestive material
  - Medical Experimentation & Bodily Autonomy: non-consensual modification, experiments on minors, captivity, interrogation, torture
  - Violence & Body Horror: graphic violence, mutilation, death, references to human remains, harm to animals and animal-coded characters
  - Cosmic & Eldritch Horror: existential dread, eldritch abominations, anomalous phenomena, cult and occult elements
  - Noir Tone: morally gray protagonists, coercive institutions, surveillance, corporate corruption, strong language
