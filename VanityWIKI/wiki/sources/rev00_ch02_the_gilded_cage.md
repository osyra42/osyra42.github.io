# Chapter 02 - The Gilded Cage

## Overview
Osyra's experience at a club where she intervenes to protect Yuki from assault, marking a pivotal moment of violent awakening and self-realization.

## Key Claims, Facts, and Dates
- Osyra arrives home in her car, crunching over gravel driveway - announcement of arrival to unresponsive house
- Derran (butler) informs her of college acceptance to first-choice college
- Osyra feels frustration despite the good news, finds Derran's formalities irritating
- She retreats to her sanctuary room in far corner of mansion where parents never venture
- Studies AP Biology, finds comfort in logical, predictable world with clear answers
- Questions why she doesn't know more about the tech she uses daily (her PC)
- Receives Facebook notification about genetically engineering cat girls video from Taylor Mitts (deemed vapid/popular)
- Irritation at incorrect terminology in video, frustration with people not doing proper research
- Reflects on being a "living trophy" - healthy, whole, brilliant child displayed but ignored by ghost-like parents
- Derran's quiet kindness noted (leaving food trays, notes about sleep) which she failed to properly thank him for
- Goes to club wearing black crop top, high-waisted jeans, leather jacket (uniform of rebellion)
- At club, observes older guy harassing Yuki (Japanese DJ with blue/pink hair) - blowing pupils wide from substances
- Guy yanks Yuki's hair back, spins her around, forearm around neck, gropes her breast through top
- Osyra experiences cold, sharp clarity - music/chatter fade, focuses on predator/prey dynamic
- Uses analytical approach: spots abandoned pool table, takes cue ball as weapon
- Approaches calmly, delivers short, brutal arc with cue ball to center of guy's face
- Impact: wet, crushing thud of cartilage/bone giving way, blood and saliva spray
- Guy releases Yuki, stumbles back, collapses in fetal position moaning/spitting teeth
- Yuki hits switch, music falters, stunned silence broken by guy's whimpers and distant bass
- Osyra stands with bloody cue ball, sees Yuki's relief/terror mixture
- Hears echo of future memory: "Picked up a cue ball and smashed his teeth in."
- Drops cue ball with heavy final thud that seems to shake room
- For first time that day, she's not the lonely rich girl/studious overachiever/polite daughter - she's a force of consequence
- Feels terrible, satisfying calm having done something that couldn't be ignored
