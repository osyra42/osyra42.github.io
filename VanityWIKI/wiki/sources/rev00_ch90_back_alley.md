# Chapter 90 - Back Alley: Final State

## Overview
Osyra, now fully embodying "Chimera One," lives as a homeless person in an alley behind a luxury department store. Her wolf traits are revealed when she receives an extraction call from her Handler. This appears to be her current/late state in the narrative timeline.

## Setting
- **Location**: Alley behind luxury department store
- **Environment**: "Thick, permanent soup of damp cardboard, old dye, and despair"
- **Conditions**: Homeless encampment, discarded items as bedding
- **Time**: Dawn, "grimy seep through canyon of brick and concrete"
- **Atmosphere**: Despair, survival, hidden identity

## Characters Present

### Osyra (Chimera One)
**Physical Appearance:**
- Long black hair in messy ponytail
- Round glasses (disguise element?)
- Brown skin smudged with dirt
- **Wolf traits revealed**:
  - Wolf ears (dark fur, "erect and alert")
  - Bushy grey wolf's tail
  - Red eyes ("striking and unsettling")
  - Enhanced physical capabilities

**Possessions:**
- Brown hooded jacket (oversized)
- Light yellow shirt
- Cheap, rugged-looking phone
- .50 caliber anti-material rifle (hidden)

**Behavior:**
- Playing a role: "nervous stray"
- "Slow, deliberate weariness" (half-feigned)
- "Coiled purpose" when called
- Moves with "impossible speed"

### Alley Residents
- **Rusty**: Homeless man, "wet, rattling cough"
- **Young woman**: Dirt-smudged cheeks, shocked by revelation
- **Others**: Unnamed, living in "cocoon of newspaper"
- **Collective reaction**: Fear, shock, silence

### Handler (Voice Only)
- **Communication**: Digital, "clipped, devoid of humanity"
- **Authority**: Military/mercenary command structure
- **Message**: 
  - "Extended period of non-contact noted"
  - "Playtime is over"
  - Orders to "rendezvous point Gamma"
  - Extraction and debrief

## Key Events

### Opening Scene
- Osyra in "nest of discarded finery"
- Dawn arrives harshly
- Other residents stirring
- Osyra already awake, listening (enhanced senses)

### The Call
- Electronic buzz from phone
- Alley goes silent (residents recognize trouble)
- Osyra answers with "small and tight" voice
- Handler requests call sign verification
- Osyra: "Chimera One"
- Orders: Proceed to Gamma for extraction
- Osyra confirms

### The Revelation
- Osyra stands with "coiled purpose"
- Retrieves hidden .50 cal rifle from beneath trash
- Alley residents shocked ("collective, sharp intake of breath")
- Pushes back hood
- Wolf ears twitch, stand erect
- Red eyes revealed
- Tail freed, gives "dismissive swish"
- Declaration: "This will be our secret"
- Exits at superhuman speed over rooftops

## Wolf Traits Displayed

### Physical
- **Ears**: Dark fur, erect, alert, twitch independently
- **Tail**: Bushy, grey, expressive ("dismissive swish")
- **Eyes**: Red, "striking and unsettling"
- **Speed**: "Impossible," leaps onto fire escape, vanishes over roofline
- **Strength**: Carries .50 cal rifle "as if it were a twig"

### Behavioral
- **Senses**: Enhanced hearing (rat scuttle, distant traffic, uneven breath)
- **Movement**: "Cat-like stretch," "coiled purpose," fluid motion
- **Presence**: Commands attention, intimidation factor
