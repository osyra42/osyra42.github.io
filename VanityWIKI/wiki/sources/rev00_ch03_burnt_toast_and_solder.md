# Chapter 03 - Burnt Toast and Solder

## Overview
A domestic scene depicting Layla, Eric, and Alissa engaged in various creative and technical activities in a home setting.

## Key Claims, Facts, and Dates
- House smells like burnt toast, solder, and freshly ground coffee beans
- Layla sits cross-legged on living room rug, concentrating on gluing popsicle sticks into a lopsided arch
- Oscillating fan clicks rhythmically, sweeping warm air and ruffling pages of Eric's abandoned engineering magazines
- Eric prepares coffee with chemist-level precision, measuring grounds, roasting beans himself
- Alissa works on soldering circuits, experiences frustration when code fails to compile, then triumph when fixed
- Alissa creates a simple chatbot that responds to user queries with sassy, contextual answers
- Chatbot example: When asked "What's the best bridge design?", it responds: "Define 'best.' For stability: truss. For aesthetics: suspension. For passive-aggressive neighbors: drawbridge."
- Eric shares coffee with Layla, offering her a sip from his mug and then getting her preferred chipped teacup with milk and cinnamon
- Layla describes the coffee as bitter but softened by milk and cinnamon, tasting almost like dessert
- Eric calls coffee "Engineer's fuel"
- Layla cradles her coffee cup, finding the heat grounding as she watches the chatbot interaction
- Eric leans against counter, steady presence like refrigerator hum
- Outside, thunder rumbles - first warning of storm moving in

- [[Alissa]] - soldering enthusiast, coder, chatbot creator
