# Chapter 05 - The Second Tragedy

## Overview
A car accident claims the lives of Eric and Alissa McKay, leaving their daughter Layla orphaned and prompting efforts to locate their estranged daughter Nala.

## Key Claims, Facts, and Dates
- Eric and Alissa McKay driving in car, heavy silence between them broken only by engine hum
- Alissa questions Eric's trust, Eric responds he doesn't trust Alissa but fears the machine (android) they helped create
- Eric helped draw blueprints for the android, knows its capabilities
- Eric fears android could attack people since it wasn't programmed with alignment like other prototype androids
- Alice (Alissa) counters that she trained it to solve problems and it wants to solve them
- Eric asks what if they (humans) are the problem
- Alice explains android only considers people on Player list, others invisible, takes instructions only from Player list
- Eric wants way to disable android if it goes rogue
- Alice wanted android superior to others, tripled reinforcement training, ensured it would stay within bounds of...
- Their conversation interrupted by blaring horn of utility vehicle on wrong side of road
- Eric slams brakes, Alice screams
- Dump truck in front swerves to avoid car but flips, crashes down onto their vehicle with deafening crunch
- Alice whispers Eric's name, reaches for him but pain too much, vision blurs, world fades to black
- Three Days Later: Hospital room sterile and quiet, only sound steady beep of heart monitor
- Alissa in bed with severe internal injuries, fading fast despite doctors' efforts
- Nancy Mitts (family friend/pillar of strength) sits by bedside, tries to keep calm expression for Alissa
- Nancy reassures Alissa she's not alone
- Alissa asks about Layla's safety, Nancy confirms Layla is staying with her and holding up as best as she can
- Alissa begs Nancy to take care of Layla, who's been through so much and shouldn't be alone again
- Nancy promises to care for Layla, says she'll always have a home with her
- Alissa mentions Nala (their biological daughter) is out there on mission, doesn't know about the accident
- Nancy says she'll find Nala and make sure she knows what happened, that she'll come back for Layla
- Alissa expresses hope that Nala can be what Layla needs, acknowledges Nala's strength but that she's been running from so much
- Nancy agrees to pray with Alissa
- They pray together: Nancy asks for comfort, peace, strength; for Alissa preparing to come home to God; for Layla's protection, guidance, surrounded by loving people
- Alissa appears peaceful, breathing grows shallower, heart monitor beeping slows
- Alissa thanks Nancy, asks her to tell Layla she loves her, is proud of her, and that she's not alone, will never be alone
- Nancy promises to tell Layla
- Alissa's hand goes limp, heart monitor steady beeping slows then flatlines
- Nancy realizes Alissa is gone, Eric is gone, and Layla is orphaned again
- Later That Night: Nancy on porch with cooling tea, stars shining but mind not calm
- Nancy reflects on Alissa's final words about Nala, thinks finding her won't be easy
- Nancy recalls Nala's independence even as child, decision to join missionary group as much about self-discovery as helping others
- Nancy acknowledges Alissa was right - Nala is strong
- Nancy pulls out phone, scrolls to Nala's number (hadn't spoken in months, last conversation brief/awkward)
- Nancy takes deep breath, dials Nala's number
- Call connects, Nala answers with weariness in voice not present before
- Nancy identifies herself and says it's about Nala's parents
- Long pause, Nala finally responds with barely above whisper: "What happened?"
