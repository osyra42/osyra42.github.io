Operation Chimera

> **Operation Chimera** is an ongoing collection of sci-fi noir short stories following a recurring cast of characters caught up in the Chimera Project — a secret program that turns people into engineered hybrid operatives. Each story follows a different mission, but the same characters (Osyra, Vanity, and the rest of the team) carry through. Expect dark themes, morally gray choices, and a slow-burn conspiracy.
