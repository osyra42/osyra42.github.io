# Keychain Knife

## Components
- Fabricated
  - 1x KUT Utility Knife
- Trinket
  - 1x Utility Blade
  - 1x Key Chain Ring

## Crafting
1. Take the KUT Utility Knife body (the 3D printed part)
2. Slide the Utility Blade into the slot on the body
3. Thread the Key Chain Ring through the loop at the end of the body
