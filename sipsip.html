# 🧋 Sip Sip A little party card game for the table. Pick some decks, draw a card, and either do what it says or take a sip. That's the whole game. > ℹ️ **Sip what?** Anything you like - it's a quiet gesture of guilt, not a shot list. Coffee, soda, water, or whatever's in your cup all count. ## ▶️ Play Sip Sip runs right in your browser - nothing to install, nothing to sign up for. **[🧋 Open Sip Sip](sipsip/index.html)** ## 🃏 How It Works 1. **Pick your decks** - turn on as many as you want; the cards get shuffled together 2. **Draw a card** - tap the draw pile to flip the top card 3. **Follow it or sip** - do what the card says, or take a sip if you'd rather not 4. **Keep going** - drawn cards move to the discard pile; play as long as the table's having fun ## 📚 The Decks Each deck is a different flavor of prompt, so you can tune the night to the room: - **Friends** - inside jokes, group-chat callouts, and light roasts - **Family** - table-safe prompts for mixed company - **Couples** - for partners playing together - **Food** - eating habits, hot takes, and guilty pleasures - **Anime** - for the weebs at the table - **Movies** - film buffs and casual watchers alike - **Games** - for the gamers - **Code** - a dev-flavored deck for the programmers > ⚠️ **Grown-ups only:** the **Buzzed** and **Sex** decks lean adult. Leave them off for family or mixed groups. ::signature::
