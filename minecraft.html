No donations required, no pay-to-win, no premium ranks — everyone gets the same experience. Hacks are allowed. PvP is consent-based. Self-care is rule #1. 🔗 [Join the Discord](https://discord.gg/2USS2tgDgE) — Ask questions, find players, or just hang out. --- ## 🆕 New to Minecraft? [Purchase Minecraft Java Edition](https://www.minecraft.net/en-us) for about $30. Any modern computer should be able to run vanilla Minecraft. For modded servers like ATM10 (~500 mods), your computer needs at least **8 GB of RAM allocated to Minecraft** (12 GB recommended, don't go over 12). A fast CPU with **3.8+ GHz** single-core speed matters more than core count. An **SSD is strongly recommended** — loading from a hard drive can take 10+ minutes. You'll need about **2 GB of free storage** for the modpack files and **Java 21** installed. --- ## 📜 Server Rules — These apply to all servers. **1. 💚 Self Care** If you need to eat, sleep, or handle real life — do it. No one will be upset that you logged off. This is a game, not a job. Take care of yourself first. **2. 🚫 Server Damage** Don't intentionally lag or crash the server. No lag machines, no chunk loaders spiraling out of control, nothing that makes it unplayable for others. This is an instant ban if this was intentional. **3. 🤝 No Disrespect** Keep it civil. Trash talk during PvP is one thing, but targeting someone personally or being excessively toxic will get you removed. People are here to relax. **4. ✋ Player Consent** Before you grief, fight, raid, or loot another player — ask them first. Players can consent to activities like war, PvP tournaments, or base raids. But if a player does not want to participate, you have to respect their decision. No exceptions. **5. 💬 Conversations** Keep chat clean. No sex, violence, politics, or hate speech. There are plenty of other places on the internet for that. This isn't one of them. **6. 🏠 Be Independent** Build your own stuff. If you want to team up, both players need to agree. Don't just move into someone's base uninvited. **7. 🔧 Hacks Allowed** Client mods are fine — X-ray, minimaps, sorting tools, shaders, whatever helps you play. The only rule is don't use them to damage the server or ruin someone else's experience without consent. For example, if you use X-ray and another player doesn't want you sharing the materials with them, you have to respect that. **8. 🗳️ Vote Ban** If a player is causing problems, the community can vote to ban them. Majority rules. This keeps the power with the players, not just the admin. **9. 📝 Changes** Any changes to the server or rules need majority approval from active players. Nobody makes unilateral decisions here. 🐛 **Reporting Issues** — Contact [@osyra42](discord:discordapp.com/users/163839418623459330) on Discord or your server's Moderators.
