# ☕ Coffee Block Community Coffee Block is my little collection of Minecraft servers — a few public ones, sometimes a private one too. They're free, Java Edition only, and running 24/7. Come and go as you like. 🔗 [Join the Discord](https://discord.gg/2USS2tgDgE) — Say hi, find players, or just hang out. --- ## 🆕 New to Minecraft? Welcome — and congrats on picking up one of the most creative games out there. Grab [Minecraft Java Edition](https://www.minecraft.net/en-us) for about $30, install it, and you're ready to play. Each server below has its own connect instructions and minimum specs. --- ## 📜 Server Rules ### **1. 💚 Self Care** If you need to eat, sleep, or handle real life — do it. No one will be upset that you logged off. This is a game, not a job. Take care of yourself first. ### **2. 🚫 Server Damage** Don't intentionally lag or crash the server. No lag machines, no chunk loaders spiraling out of control, nothing that makes it unplayable for others. This is an instant ban if this was intentional. ### **3. 🤝 No Disrespect** Keep it civil. Trash talk during PvP is one thing, but targeting someone personally or being excessively toxic will get you removed. People are here to relax. ### **4. 💬 Conversations** Keep chat clean. No sex, violence, politics, or hate speech. There are plenty of other places on the internet for that. This isn't one of them. ### **5. ✋ Player Consent** Before you grief, fight, raid, loot, or steal from another player — ask first. Consent can be given for war, PvP, raids, or sharing resources. If they say no, respect it. No exceptions. ### **6. 🏠 Be Independent** Build your own stuff. If you want to team up, both players need to agree. Don't just move into someone's base uninvited. ### **7. 🔧 Hacks Allowed** **Only on servers that permit it** (check each server's Rules line). Client mods are fine — X-ray, minimaps, sorting tools, shaders, whatever helps you play. The only catch is don't use them to damage the server or ruin someone else's experience without consent. For example, if you use X-ray and another player doesn't want you sharing the materials with them, you have to respect that. > 🐛 **Reporting Issues** — Contact [@osyra42](discord:discordapp.com/users/163839418623459330) on Discord or your server's Moderators.
