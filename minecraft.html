# ☕ Coffee Block Community **Coffee Block is my collection of Minecraft servers.** The public ones have no whitelist, no application, no waiting — just connect and play. I also host private servers occasionally. All are free, **Java Edition only**, and run 24/7. No donations required, no pay-to-win, no premium ranks — everyone gets the same experience. These aren't cheap cloud VMs. I own the hardware, host the files, and handle every bit of administration myself. I also occasionally log in to play. Fourteen years of experience hosting means the servers stay up, the rules make sense, and the community comes first. Hacks are allowed on any public server I host. PvP is consent-based. Self-care is rule #1. 🔗 [Join the Discord](https://discord.gg/2USS2tgDgE) — Ask questions, find players, or just hang out. --- ## 🆕 New to Minecraft? [Purchase Minecraft Java Edition](https://www.minecraft.net/en-us) for about $30. Any modern computer should be able to run vanilla Minecraft. For ATM10 (~500 mods), these are the minimum specs you need to even log in: - 🧠 **CPU:** 4 cores at 3.0+ GHz (single-thread speed matters most) - 🎮 **GPU:** GTX 1050 Ti / 4 GB VRAM or equivalent - 💾 **RAM:** 16 GB total system; allocate 8–10 GB to Minecraft (don't go over 12) - 🗃️ **SSD:** Required — HDD causes very long load times - 📁 **Disk space:** ~15 GB free for modpack files - ☕ **Java:** Version 21 (required) --- ## 📜 Server Rules ### **1. 💚 Self Care** If you need to eat, sleep, or handle real life — do it. No one will be upset that you logged off. This is a game, not a job. Take care of yourself first. ### **2. 🚫 Server Damage** Don't intentionally lag or crash the server. No lag machines, no chunk loaders spiraling out of control, nothing that makes it unplayable for others. This is an instant ban if this was intentional. ### **3. 🤝 No Disrespect** Keep it civil. Trash talk during PvP is one thing, but targeting someone personally or being excessively toxic will get you removed. People are here to relax. ### **4. 💬 Conversations** Keep chat clean. No sex, violence, politics, or hate speech. There are plenty of other places on the internet for that. This isn't one of them. ### **5. ✋ Player Consent** Before you grief, fight, raid, loot, or steal from another player — ask first. Consent can be given for war, PvP, raids, or sharing resources. If they say no, respect it. No exceptions. ### **6. 🏠 Be Independent** Build your own stuff. If you want to team up, both players need to agree. Don't just move into someone's base uninvited. ### **7. 🔧 Hacks Allowed** **Only on servers that permit it** (check each server's Rules line). Client mods are fine — X-ray, minimaps, sorting tools, shaders, whatever helps you play. The only catch is don't use them to damage the server or ruin someone else's experience without consent. For example, if you use X-ray and another player doesn't want you sharing the materials with them, you have to respect that. > 🐛 **Reporting Issues** — Contact [@osyra42](discord:discordapp.com/users/163839418623459330) on Discord or your server's Moderators.
