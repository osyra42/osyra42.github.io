# ☕ Coffee Block Community **Join right now.** No whitelist, no application, no waiting. Just connect and play. All servers are free, **Java Edition only**, and open to everyone 24/7. No donations required, no pay-to-win, no premium ranks — everyone gets the same experience. These aren't cheap cloud VMs. I run real hardware, I'm the admin, and I actually play. 14 years of hosting means the servers stay up, the rules make sense, and the community comes first. Hacks are allowed. PvP is consent-based. Self-care is rule #1. 🔗 [Join the Discord](https://discord.gg/2USS2tgDgE) — Ask questions, find players, or just hang out. --- ## 🆕 New to Minecraft? [Purchase Minecraft Java Edition](https://www.minecraft.net/en-us) for about $30. Any modern computer should be able to run vanilla Minecraft. For modded servers like ATM10 (~500 mods), your computer needs at least **8 GB of RAM allocated to Minecraft** (12 GB recommended, don't go over 12). A fast CPU with **3.8+ GHz** single-core speed matters more than core count. An **SSD is strongly recommended** — loading from a hard drive can take 10+ minutes. You'll need about **2 GB of free storage** for the modpack files and **Java 21** installed. --- ## 📜 Server Rules ### **1. 💚 Self Care** If you need to eat, sleep, or handle real life — do it. No one will be upset that you logged off. This is a game, not a job. Take care of yourself first. ### **2. 🚫 Server Damage** Don't intentionally lag or crash the server. No lag machines, no chunk loaders spiraling out of control, nothing that makes it unplayable for others. This is an instant ban if this was intentional. ### **3. 🤝 No Disrespect** Keep it civil. Trash talk during PvP is one thing, but targeting someone personally or being excessively toxic will get you removed. People are here to relax. ### **4. 💬 Conversations** Keep chat clean. No sex, violence, politics, or hate speech. There are plenty of other places on the internet for that. This isn't one of them. ### **5. ✋ Player Consent** Before you grief, fight, raid, loot, or steal from another player — ask them first. Players can consent to activities like war, PvP tournaments, base raids, or sharing resources. But if a player does not want to participate, you have to respect their decision. No exceptions. ### **6. 🏠 Be Independent** Build your own stuff. If you want to team up, both players need to agree. Don't just move into someone's base uninvited. ### **7. 🔧 Hacks Allowed\*** Client mods are fine — X-ray, minimaps, sorting tools, shaders, whatever helps you play. The only rule is don't use them to damage the server or ruin someone else's experience without consent. For example, if you use X-ray and another player doesn't want you sharing the materials with them, you have to respect that. > 🐛 **Reporting Issues** — Contact [@osyra42](discord:discordapp.com/users/163839418623459330) on Discord or your server's Moderators.
