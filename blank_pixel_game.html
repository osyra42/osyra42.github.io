# 🕹️ Blank Pixel Game A work-in-progress pixel art adventure built in GameMaker Studio 2 by osyra42. Explore the world, collect coins, and fight your way through — more to come! 🎮 **[PLAY IN BROWSER](blank_pixel_game/index.html)** > Press F11 for fullscreen, makes it a much better experience. --- ## 📦 Releases ### 🆕 2026.06.06 — Minimap & Rarity A minimap now sits in the top-right corner, drawing the tiles, enemies, loot, and the ladder around you — with the floor count tucked right below it. Loot got a proper rarity system too: six tiers from Common all the way up to Unique, color-coded the way you'd expect from your favorite loot games. Tooltips were upgraded to match — hover an inventory item and you'll see its name in its rarity color, the rarity tier, and a description. There's a version display down in the lower-right corner now, and the floor counter moved up beside the minimap. Fixed two annoyances: the player no longer spawns inside a pot or an enemy when entering a fresh dungeon, and loot dropped by the same enemy lands on separate tiles instead of piling up on one. **Assets** - **Portable** — [blank_pixel_game@2026.06.06-WINZIP.zip](assets/downloads/blank_pixel_game/[osyra42]%20blank_pixel_game@2026.06.06-WINZIP.zip) - **Windows** — [blank_pixel_game@2026.06.06-WINDOWS.zip](assets/downloads/blank_pixel_game/[osyra42]%20blank_pixel_game@2026.06.06-WINDOWS.zip) - **Browser** — [blank_pixel_game@2026.06.06-HTML5.zip](assets/downloads/blank_pixel_game/[osyra42]%20blank_pixel_game@2026.06.06-HTML5.zip) - **Linux** — [blank_pixel_game@2026.06.06-LINUX.zip](assets/downloads/blank_pixel_game/[osyra42]%20blank_pixel_game@2026.06.06-LINUX.zip) >>> 📦 2026.05.31 — Pause Menu ESC opens a pause menu with Resume and Quit buttons. Every floor is a fresh 64×64 layout — never the same twice - Share a seed to replay the same dungeon ### ⚔️ Combat - Directional sword swing - Enemies with health bars and floating damage numbers - Breakable pots — 150 scattered through every dungeon; smash for a chance at coins ### 🪜 Floor Progression - Clear all enemies and a ladder appears, taking you to a freshly-generated dungeon - Floor counter ticks up each descent ### 🎒 Inventory & Loot - Six rarity tiers from Common to Unique, color-coded; tooltips show an item's name in its rarity color, the tier, and a description - Sorting and equipment management - Closest-only pickup — only the nearest in-range item is grabbed - Enemies drop coins on death; lucky coins worth more than the loot pool show in green ### 💬 Chat-driven HUD - Minimap in the top-right showing nearby tiles, enemies, loot, and the ladder, with the floor count below it - Scrolling chat log with unique identity colors per object - Messages fade after a moment instead of sticking -
