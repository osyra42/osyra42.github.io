# 📖 Operation Chimera > **Operation Chimera** is an ongoing collection of sci-fi noir stories. The cast keeps getting pulled into the Chimera Project - a secret program that turns people into engineered hybrid operatives. It's dark, the choices aren't clean, and the conspiracy unwinds slowly. --- ## 📚 Stories Self-contained.
