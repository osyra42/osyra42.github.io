# About Me I'm a multidisciplinary digital creator who bridges code, art, and storytelling into cohesive experiences. Over the past eight years, I've built everything from AI-powered Discord bots with VTuber avatars to interactive educational tools, custom 3D-printed designs, and published fantasy novels. My work spans web development, 3D character modeling and rigging, procedural worksheet generators, Minecraft server management, and AI integration—all designed, built, and maintained solo. What drives me is the challenge of making complex systems accessible and expressive. Whether I'm training an AI to respond with personality, architecting a Discord bot that's evolved through eight major iterations, designing canvas-based math worksheets for teachers, or creating fully-rigged VTuber models, I approach each project as an opportunity to solve real problems while exploring new technical frontiers.
