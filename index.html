> 🎨 Expanding into visual work — Blender, 3D rendering and animation, and multimedia video for YouTube. Code and dev work continues alongside it. # 👋 About Me I'm a self-taught developer who builds whatever catches my interest — AI bots, games, websites, educational tools, stories, 3D models, you name it. I've been at this for about ten years, doing everything solo. My Discord bot [Vanity](vanity.html) has gone through 9 major rewrites. I run Minecraft servers, write fantasy novels, and build worksheet generators for homeschool families. If it sounds like a lot, it is. I like staying busy. Lately I've been pulling more toward visual work — 3D, animation, and video editing — alongside the usual dev work. --- # 🛠️ What I Build ### **3D & Animation** I'm learning Blender — 3D modeling, rendering, and animation. This is newer territory for me, and a lot of my creative energy is going here lately. ### **Mixed Multimedia** Combining audio, video, and imagery made through different methods — live action, animation, and more — into single pieces for [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@osyra42). Mixing modes in one video lets a story do things a single format can't. ### **Developer Tools** I build utilities when I need them. Media Mimic is a Python app I made to organize media files. I also maintain a collection of [code resources](code_resources.html) for anyone trying to learn. ### **Minecraft Server** I run a [Minecraft server](minecraft.html) free of charge with 14 years of hosting experience. I handle everything from server management and modpack configuration to community moderation. ### **Game Development** I'm building [A Sundered Gem](blank_pixel_game.html), a pixel art dungeon crawler in GameMaker Studio 2 inspired by Diablo 1. It features a roguelike death loop, loot tables driven by enemy difficulty, and a story about reassembling a shattered gem to vanquish a trapped demon. Available to play in browser or download for Windows. ### **Creative Writing** I write sci-fi and fantasy — Infinite Devastation, Ever Diamond, and my ongoing series [Operation Chimera](operation_chimera.html). If you like weird worlds and morally gray characters, you might enjoy them. ### **Educational Tools** I've developed interactive canvas-based [worksheets](worksheets.html) for arithmetic, measurements, and currency counting. These tools generate randomized problems with multi-page print support, making them perfect for teachers and homeschooling parents. ### **AI & Automation** [Vanity](vanity.html) is my AI agent — she does voice recognition, voice output, online research, file editing, note taking, and Minecraft automation. She's gone through 9 major rewrites and is currently on the Kale update. --- # 🎯 My Approach This website started as a crayon drawing on a piece of paper, mocked up in Photoshop.
