# 👋 About Me > "A jack of all trades is a master of none, but oftentimes better than a master of one." > — William Shakespeare I'm a **Jack-of-all-trades VTuber** — a one-person variety act who'd rather be decent at a dozen things than great at just one. I've been self-taught for about ten years and I do all of it solo: building games, writing sci-fi and fantasy, modeling and animating in 3D, cutting videos, tinkering with AI, and running a Minecraft server. I stream on Discord, Twitch, and YouTube, with videos on YouTube between sessions. If it sounds like a lot, it is — I just like staying busy. Come hang out. 🔞 This is an adult space — open, not safe. Banter and blunt honesty are the culture here; malicious intent isn't. Know the difference, be a grown-up about it, and we'll get along great. **✅ Do:** thick skin, dark humor, blunt honesty, unpopular opinions, giving as good as you get **🚫 Don't:** malicious intent, manufactured outrage, thin skin, daycare energy, kid gloves --- # 🧰 A Bit of Everything A running dump of everything I'm good at — tech, hands-on, creative, whatever. No filter and no affiliation; if I can do it, it goes here. **💻 Tech** — web development (HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Canvas), Python, PHP, SQLite, REST APIs, AI/LLM integration & prompt engineering, game development (GameMaker Studio 2), 3D modeling, rigging & animation (Blender), VTuber/VRM avatars, 3D printing, video editing & multimedia, Minecraft server administration, Git/GitHub **✍️ Creative** — sci-fi & fantasy fiction, worldbuilding, technical writing, digital storytelling, UI/UX design --- # 🎯 My Approach I do everything myself — design, code, write, render, deploy, fix bugs at 2am. The hardest part of any project isn't the skill, it's starting it; the second hardest is keeping the momentum; the third is telling yourself you did a good job.
