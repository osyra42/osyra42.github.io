# 🧊 Blender Resources 🎯 The fastest way to get good at Blender is to **finish a project.** Pick something - a coffee mug, a low-poly room, a character bust - and follow it through to a render. Everything you need to learn comes up along the way. Here are the resources I lean on, plus the ones everyone learning Blender ends up pointed at. 🚀 🌐 **[blender.org](https://www.blender.org/)** - the official site. Free, open source, runs on Windows, Mac, and Linux. 📖 **[Blender Manual](https://docs.blender.org/manual/en/latest/)** - the reference docs. Searchable, indexed, kept current with each release. > ℹ️ I'm on **Blender 5.1.1** at the time of writing. Most tutorials below were made on earlier versions - the fundamentals translate, but UI labels and shortcut placements may have shifted. --- ## 🍩 Start Here The donut tutorial is the starting point everyone points to - by the time you finish it you've touched modeling, materials, lighting, and rendering. - [Blender Guru: Donut Tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=z-Xl9tGqH14) - Andrew Price's beginner series - [Grant Abbitt: Blender for Beginners](https://www.youtube.com/@grantabbitt) - friendly, methodical, project-based --- ## 🎬 YouTube Channels - [Blender Guru](https://www.youtube.com/@blenderguru) - beginner to intermediate, lots of project tutorials - [Grant Abbitt](https://www.youtube.com/@grantabbitt) - beginner-friendly, modeling and game-asset focus - [Default Cube](https://www.youtube.com/@DefaultCube) - geometry nodes and procedural workflows - [CG Cookie](https://www.youtube.com/@cg_cookie) - broader CG education, much of it free - [Imphenzia](https://www.youtube.com/@Imphenzia) - low-poly modeling, fast workflows --- ## 🪨 Modeling - [Blender Manual: Modeling](https://docs.blender.org/manual/en/latest/modeling/index.html) - [Josh Gambrell - Hard Surface Fundamentals](https://www.youtube.com/@JoshGambrell) --- ## 🦴 Rigging - [Blender Manual: Armatures](https://docs.blender.org/manual/en/latest/animation/armatures/index.html) --- ## 🎞️ Animation - [Blender Manual: Animation](https://docs.blender.org/manual/en/latest/animation/index.html) --- ## 🌍 Asset Libraries - [Polyhaven](https://polyhaven.com/) - free HDRIs, textures, and models (CC0) - [BlenderKit](https://www.blenderkit.com/) - free and paid asset library, integrates inside Blender - [Sketchfab](https://sketchfab.com/) - large community model library - [Blender Studio](https://studio.blender.org/) - production assets and open-movie files from the Blender Foundation --- ## 🏘️ Communities - [Blender Artists Forum](https://blenderartists.org/) - long-running community - [r/blender](https://www.reddit.com/r/blender/) - subreddit, lots of WIP threads and feedback - [BlenderNation](https://www.blendernation.com/) - news and tutorials roundup --- ::signature::
