# 🤖 Vanity [Vanity's Legal Stuff](vanity_legal.html) Vanity has been split into two products that share the lineage below: **Vanity Minecraft** (the in-game bot that plays alongside you) and **Vanity Kale** (the AI agent for voice, file editing, and research). --- ## Vanity Minecraft *Active* An autonomous Minecraft bot that picks a goal and works backward from it. The current goal is fishing — find water, secure a fishing rod, fish, manage the haul, and defend the spot. **Behavior:** - **Goal-Oriented Autonomy** — Logs into the server and identifies what it needs to fish. Locates water and checks whether it already has a fishing rod or the materials for one (sticks + string) - **Backward Crafting** — If it lacks the rod, it walks the chain backward: sticks need planks, planks need logs. It cuts down a tree and crafts forward (logs → planks → sticks → fishing rod), building a crafting bench along the way if it doesn't have one - **Chest Awareness** — If it spotted a chest earlier in the world, it checks the chest for any of those materials before gathering from scratch - **Storage Management** — Once fishing, when its inventory fills up, it crafts a chest, places it near the fishing spot, and offloads the haul into it. Then it goes back to fishing - **Threat Defense** — If something attacks it or threatens its fishing spot during the session, it stops fishing and fights off the threat - **Player Reputation** — Remembers how individual players have treated it. A player who's hurt the bot before gets attacked on sight. A player who's given it crafting materials gets recognized as friendly — instead of fighting, the bot offers them a rare fishing item --- ## Kale Update *MAR 2026* Vanity's biggest update yet.
