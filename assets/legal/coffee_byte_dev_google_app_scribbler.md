# Privacy Policy for Scribbler

**Last updated:** March 5, 2026

## Introduction
This Privacy Policy describes how Scribbler ("we," "our," or "us") handles your information when you use our Google Workspace application. We believe in complete transparency: **Our application is designed to collect no personal information.**

## Data Collection
Scribbler **does not collect, store, or process any personal data, user content, or usage statistics.**

- **No User Information:** We do not access, read, or store your name, email address, or any profile information associated with your Google account.
- **No Content Access:** The app does not read, modify, or store any files, documents, or emails from your Google Drive, Gmail, or any other Google service.
- **No Analytics:** We do not use any third-party analytics or crash reporting tools that might collect data about how you use the app.

## Permissions
Because the app collects no data, it requires no special OAuth consent scopes (permissions) to access your Google Account information or data. The app functions without needing to "see" or "manage" your personal files or account details.

## Children's Privacy
Our application does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not collect personally identifiable information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top.

## Your Consent
By using our app, you consent to this Privacy Policy. Since we collect no data, there are no privacy settings for you to manage within the app.
