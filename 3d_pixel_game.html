# 🎮 3D Pixel Game A low-poly 3D platformer with pixel-art textures and combat, built in **Godot 4.7**. Think Nintendo 64 era - chunky models, crunchy pixel textures, and readable-over-realistic. If you can tell it's an apple, it's good enough. 🎮 **PLAY IN BROWSER** — *coming soon, once the first web build is ready* > The playable browser version will live here as soon as there's a Godot HTML5 export to drop in. --- ::toc:: ## 🎨 The Look — "N64-ish" The retro feel comes from a handful of deliberate choices, not high detail: - **Low-poly meshes** - few triangles, flat faces, simple silhouettes - **Pixel-art textures** - hand-drawn low-res texture sheets wrapped on the 3D models - **Nearest-neighbor filtering** - textures stay crisp and blocky instead of blurred - **Low-res render** - the frame is rendered small and scaled up for that chunky pixel density - **Simple lighting** - vertex-lit or unshaded materials read more retro than modern PBR ## ✨ Planned Features ### 🕹️ 3D Platforming - Run, jump, and gravity with a third-person follow camera - Platformer feel: coyote time, jump buffering, and air control ### ⚔️ Combat - Hitboxes and hurtboxes, health, and hit reactions - Enemies with basic behavior - patrol, chase, attack ## 🧭 Status This one is still in the design stage - notes first, build second.
