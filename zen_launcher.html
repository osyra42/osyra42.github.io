Zen Launcher

Zen Launcher builds small launcher scripts (`.bat` for Windows, `.sh` for Mac/Linux) that sit next to your program. When someone runs them, they check that the right things are installed first — Python, Java, whatever you need — and only launch your program once the coast is clear. No more cryptic "command not found" errors for your users.
