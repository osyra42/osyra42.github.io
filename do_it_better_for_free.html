Free Software Alternatives Guide

A comprehensive guide to free and open-source software (FOSS) alternatives.

WHY FOSS?
├─ No recurring costs or subscriptions
├─ Enhanced transparency (open source code)
├─ Faster innovation and bug fixes
├─ Greater customization options
└─ Freedom from vendor lock-in

1. Desktop & Productivity

OFFICE SUITES (replaces Microsoft 365, Google Workspace)
├─ LibreOffice ────────── Full suite, free for commercial use
├─ Microsoft 365 Free ─── Web-based Word, Excel, PowerPoint
└─ WPS Office ─────────── Slick suite with free PDF reader

PDF MANAGEMENT (replaces Adobe Acrobat)
└─ PDF-XChange Editor ── Editing, digital signatures

PASSWORD MANAGEMENT (replaces 1Password, LastPass)
├─ Bitwarden ──────────── Open-source, strong security
├─ KeePass ────────────── Password generator, 2FA, portable
└─ Padloc, Passbolt ──── Team-focused options

WRITING & GRAMMAR (replaces Grammarly)
└─ LanguageTool ───────── Browser extension, 30+ languages

FILE COMPRESSION (replaces WinZip, WinRAR)
└─ 7-Zip ──────────────── Powerful archiver, numerous formats

Notes

WHY CHOOSE OPEN-SOURCE?
├─ Transparency ────── Publicly accessible code
├─ Community ──────── Community-driven innovation
├─ Freedom ─────────── No vendor lock-in
└─ Cost ─────────────── Most are free for personal and commercial use

BEFORE YOU SWITCH
├─ Check system requirements
├─ Review licensing terms (especially for commercial use)
└─ Most tools listed are free for both personal and commercial use
