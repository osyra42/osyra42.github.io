# Principles — planning map

*The interpersonal principles Osyra demonstrates in the field, and how each one gets thrown back at her. This is the vignette-planning tool: pick a principle, show it WORKING as genuine kindness, then show the cruelty returned. The principles themselves are common self-help ideas (unprotectable) - the scenarios, framing, and structure here are all original to this book.*

Per-vignette beat: **she uses the principle → it works, connection happens → cruelty returned → the undertow ("they'd unite if I just let the monsters come... that's not her job").**

---

## Fundamentals of handling people

| Principle | What it is | How Osyra uses it (kindness) | How it's repaid (cruelty) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Don't criticize or condemn | Blame breeds defensiveness, never change | She doesn't blame a panicking civilian for freezing, or a survivor for running | They blame *her* — for being late, for what she is, for the ones she couldn't save |
| Give honest appreciation | People starve to feel important | She tells a terrified witness their info saved lives — and means it | They take the credit, then call her the monster to the next crowd |
| Arouse an eager want | Frame it around their desire | She gets a holdout to evacuate by speaking to what *they* want to protect | Once safe, they demand more, resent the cost, give nothing back |

## Making people trust / like her

| Principle | What it is | How Osyra uses it | How it's repaid |
|---|---|---|---|
| Be genuinely interested | Care, and they care back | She learns the names of the people in a shelter, asks after them | They flinch when she remembers — "how does it *know* me?" |
| Smile / warmth | An honest glad-to-see-you | She keeps her voice and face soft to disarm fear | The warmth reads as a predator's trick; they trust the armed human who sneers instead |
| Remember the name | Sweetest sound to a person | She uses a child's name to calm them mid-attack | The parents later scrub her from the story entirely |
| Be a good listener | Attention is a high compliment | She lets a broken survivor talk themselves calm | They spill everything, then testify against her kind |
| Talk to their interests | Meet them where they are | She connects over a civilian's small ordinary life | They use the opening to beg a favor, then vanish |
| Make them feel important | Treat people as if they matter | She gives dignity to someone everyone else wrote off | That person turns cruelest of all the moment they have power over someone weaker |

## Winning them to her side

| Principle | What it is | How Osyra uses it | How it's repaid |
|---|---|---|---|
| Avoid arguments | You can't win one | She defuses a mob instead of countering it | They "win" by turning on each other the second she leaves |
| Never say "you're wrong" | Saves the dignity | She lets a wrong, frightened leader keep face to prevent panic | He rewrites it as *his* plan and brands her the threat |
| Admit your own faults first | Disarms them | She owns a mission she couldn't finish | They use the admission as the whole indictment |
| See it from their view | Understand the why | She understands why they fear her and doesn't punish it | Understanding is one-way; no one asks what it costs her |
| Appeal to nobler motives | Give them a reputation to live up to | She calls a crowd braver/kinder than they are, to make them so | They rise to it *for the cameras*, then loot each other in the dark |

## Leading without resentment

| Principle | What it is | How Osyra uses it | How it's repaid |
|---|---|---|---|
| Praise, then correct indirectly | No humiliation | She corrects a reckless volunteer gently, in private | He tells everyone she "threatened" him |
| Let them save face | Never humiliate | She lets a coward pretend he held the line | He's promoted to command the next hunt — of her |
| Praise every improvement | Encourage the small step | She notices a hostile town softening, says so | The softening lasts exactly until the danger's gone |
| A reputation to live up to | Name the better self | She treats them as the people they could be | They prove, again, they'd rather be the other thing |

---

## The turn that closes the book

Every entry above proves the same equation: **the principles WORK — people are genuinely disarmed, calmed, helped — and it changes nothing about what they do to each other.** Kindness is effective and thankless in the same breath.

And under all of it, the truth she won't say aloud: a shared monster is the one thing that would make them a team. She could unite humanity by failing. **"That's not our job."**
