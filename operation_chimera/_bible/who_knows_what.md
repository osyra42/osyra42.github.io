# Who knows what

Tracks who knows Osyra is Chimeran and who knows her history - and since when. Continuity trap: a character reacting to her ears in a scene where they already knew, or vice versa.

## Knows she is Chimeran (sees/knows the ears, tail, red eyes)
- **Uta** - yes, since ~18. Keeps it to herself by choice/loyalty.
- **The Agency / Vanity / Korval / Layla** - yes. It's her job; they handle her as an Operator.
- **Titan Reach (Vasquez, Thorne, evaluators)** - yes; the reveal is the point of the Borne Weapon application.
- **Rosalyn** - NOT until Blood Relation ch4 (present, age 29), when she walks in on Osyra's true form. Before that Osyra wears the full disguise around her. After: accepts it fully, examines the modifications as a doctor.

## Does NOT know / kept hidden
- **Biological parents (up north)** - unknown whether they know about the modifications. As of Blood Relation ch9 (the knock), Osyra has NOT revealed herself to them. Flag any draft that assumes they already know.
- **Civilian world generally** - she conceals traits (contacts, hats, clothing) for safety. This is social concealment, not a legal gag.

## Secrecy nature (important for proofreading tone)
- In the EXISTING stories, her silence about the lab/Agency reads as **operational discretion + safety + shame**. In-story lines like "can't talk about who or how," "it's military" = this discretion.
- The NEW online-posts A-plot (see `planned_arc.md`) introduces an **NDA** she posts after - Titan Reach is who she exposes. Treat NDA references in that telling as intended, NOT as inconsistencies to flag. The two are different narrative frames, not a contradiction to "fix."

## The photograph thread (Blood Relation)
- Osyra takes the photo of the young woman (her mother) off a wall in an abandoned house MONTHS before ch1. Vanity IDs it as Rosalyn's line in ch2. Keep that lead-time consistent.
