# Names & spellings

Canonical forms. If a draft differs, flag it.

## People
- **Osyra** - protagonist. Surname **Renaya** (from the father she never met; shared with grandmother Rosalyn). Callsign **Chimera One** / "Chimera-9" refers to her serum, not her callsign - keep those distinct.
- **Rosalyn Renaya** - grandmother. Pediatrician, age 68 (present). Met Osyra for the first time at present-day (Osyra 29).
- **Korval** - Osyra's driver/bodyguard. American, born to Russian immigrant parents (the "Russian" read in-story is Osyra's, not his nationality).
- **Vanity** - AI android handler, codename **Wiki** (keeps the record). Default to "Vanity" (informal); "Wiki" only in formal events/briefings.
- **Layla Null** - assists Vanity; the human-touch / emotional-support role.
- **Uta** - bartender who took Osyra in (~age 18); knows she's Chimeran, keeps it to herself.
- **Alissa** - (wiki character; confirm role when proofreading her scenes)
- **Eric** - (wiki character; confirm role when proofreading his scenes)
- **Miranda Vasquez, MD** - Titan Reach medical screening director (Borne Weapon).
- **Thorne** - Titan Reach figure who makes the offer (Borne Weapon ch5).

## Terms / places
- **Chimera-9** - hyphenated. Osyra's serum/virus variant. One of a numbered shelf of DNA-splicing versions.
- **Titan Reach** - two words. Through-line ANTAGONIST: funded the lab, recruited/trained her, surveils the Agency, stalks Chimerans for research. See `titan_reach.md`. (Not merely a Borne Weapon recruiter.)
- **Aurora Pulse** - location (wiki).
- **Hunting lodge** - Osyra's agency-issued cover home.
- **The Agency** - her current employer. Operates quietly, files everything.
- **Hadley's Fork** - rural town up north (Blood Relation ch9). Apostrophe-s.

## Drift watch (easy mistakes)
- Chimera-9 (serum) vs Chimera One (callsign) - do not conflate
- Renaya not Renaya-variants
- No "Aunt Marissa" - NO aunt exists in canon. Raised by poor biological parents to age 6, then foster homes. Flag any aunt reference.
