# _bible - internal consistency reference

NOT forward-facing. Nothing links here; it is never published. This is the raw continuity ledger used to **proofread** Operation Chimera drafts - blunt facts the public `wiki/` smooths over: exact ages, name spellings, the flat timeline, who-knows-what-when, physical details.

Osyra writes the fiction. These docs are the net that catches inconsistencies.

## Files
- `names_and_spellings.md` - canonical spelling of every name/term; watch-list of easy-to-drift forms
- `timeline.md` - flat chronological ledger keyed to Osyra's age (dates are NOT fixed in canon)
- `who_knows_what.md` - which characters know Osyra is Chimeran / know her history, and since when
- `physical_facts.md` - Osyra's body and disguise details that must stay consistent
- `legal_premise.md` - Titan Reach vs Osyra: the through-line legal argument (dangerousness-as-pretext reclamation) and why it self-destructs

## How to use when proofreading
1. Read the draft.
2. Check every name, age, date-reference, and "who knows X" claim against these files.
3. Flag conflicts - do not rewrite the prose. Report: draft says A, bible says B.
4. When canon is newly established in an approved draft, update the relevant bible file.

Seeded 2026-07-23 by extracting from the existing wiki + stories. Correct anything wrong - the bible is authoritative once you've vetted it.
