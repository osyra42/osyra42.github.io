# Planned arc - the online-posts narrative (A-plot)

Source: author's own plan (2026-07-23). This is a **new narrative approach**, not a retcon of the existing stories - a slightly different angle where the book is framed as Osyra's online posts. Proofread upcoming sections against this intent.

## The framing
- Book opens with **Osyra making online posts** - talking about her family, the lab, etc. A different narrative angle for the A-plot (the confessional/serial voice the stories already carry).
- She speaks about **Titan Reach after an NDA** (an NDA exists in this telling; the posting happens once she's free to talk). See NDA note below.

## The arc (sequence)
1. She starts talking about it **online** - specifically Titan Reach and the inhumane experiment (super-soldier program). Realistic medical/training-recruitment texture.
2. **No traction at first** - the posts go nowhere.
3. Eventually **Titan Reach finds out** and moves to **reclaim her as property** - arguing she is a dangerous engineered asset that must revert to their custody/control. Dangerousness is the pretext; ownership is the goal. See `legal_premise.md` - this is the through-line legal argument for most of the story.
4. **She wins.** The claim was never plausible, and pressing it forces the buried program into open court. As a result, **Chimerans can be open in society.**
5. She **receives all merit awards** (previously withheld) - route through the court judgment / an outside body, NOT Titan Reach graciously handing them over.
6. She **leaves the sub-branch / the Agency and goes independent.**
