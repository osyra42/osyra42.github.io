# Physical facts - Osyra

Body and disguise details that must stay consistent across drafts.

## Chimeran traits (Chimera-9, wolf splice)
- **Ears** - wolf ears, proper fur. They regulate heat fine and twitch to a room's temperature on their own. NOT a defect to manage. Medical interest (Rosalyn) lives at the human-wolf INTERFACE (jaw, skin transitions), not the wolf features themselves.
- **Tail** - present; "the only part of me that has never learned to lie" (tells on her). Curls around chair legs, twitches with feeling.
- **Eyes** - red, NOT slit. Round pupils. The Chimera gene does not create slit eyes. (The Red Light partner has cat-slit red eyes because he's a different variant - do not carry his eye shape over to Osyra.)
- **Fur/coloring** - black fur against black hair (Borne Weapon ch1).
- **Height** - six foot four (6'4"). Large enough that men "measure themselves against it."

## Disguise (civilian / around those who don't know)
- Contacts (hide red eyes), hat (hide ears), clothing/dress. A "costume" she resents.
- The private "dog shake" after showering - a voluntary body thing she enjoys and hides.
- Wears it "for the mission".

## Voice / temperament (for flagging OOC prose)
- First person, retrospective, analytical, clipped, deadpan, darkly funny. Names her own tells, catalogues exits, "runs arithmetic."
- Dark, brooding; doesn't speak much until necessary; rarely sociable; haunted by her past, rarely discusses it. A voluntary open confessional is a stretch and needs in-story motivation.

## Drinks
- Chamomile, NOT coffee. Rosalyn instinctively makes chamomile for her; notes/accepts the coffee-avoidance without asking why (Blood Relation ch3, ch6).
