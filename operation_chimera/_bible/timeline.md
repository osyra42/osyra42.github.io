# Timeline

Dates are NOT fixed in canon - track SEQUENCE, keyed to Osyra's age. Present = age 29 (~9 years an Operator).

| Age | Event | Source |
|-----|-------|--------|
| 0-6 | Born to extremely poor biological parents; kept "on nothing" until 6. Grandmother Rosalyn in the phone book, never contacted. | chr_osyra |
| 6 | A school nurse files a report (too thin, too quiet); the STATE takes her. (Not a voluntary "given up.") | chr_osyra, history |
| 6-16 | Foster care - a few homes, "each colder than the last." No photos of her exist after this point. | history |

| 16 | Injected with Chimera-9; stabilizes, keeps her mind; most other kids die or come out worse. External force attacks the facility; explosions; she escapes; written off as industrial accident. | history |

| 29 (present) | Blood Relation: meets Rosalyn for the first time; Rosalyn inspects her ears (first hybrid patient); gives her the mother's letter; drives north. | history, blood_relation |

## Ordering flags for proofreading
- Homelessness comes AFTER the lab (age 16-18), not before. A "family -> lab -> homeless" telling is correct on that axis but must not skip the 6-16 foster decade.
- Rosalyn NEVER raised her; they meet only at 29.
- "No photographs exist after age 6" - Rosalyn's Polaroids at 29 matter because of this. Flag any earlier photo of Osyra.
