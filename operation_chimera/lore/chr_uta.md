# Uta Yuki

![Uta Yuki](operation_chimera/lore/imgs/uta0.jpg)

## Current
She owns a nightclub called **Aurora Pulse** that Osyra often hangs out at. She used to be the primary DJ there and eventually bought the place from the previous owner. She still DJs just because it's fun. More of a lifestyle character than a combat one. Into music and tech.

She has not yet appeared.

## Background

### The bartender who took Osyra in
Before Aurora Pulse, in an earlier era, Uta worked as an energetic bartender at a small dance club. That's where she crossed paths with Osyra - homeless, half-feral, worn down by the better part of two years living rough after the lab (it started in the woods and became a long homeless drift). Uta didn't flinch at her and didn't ask questions.

Their whole relationship starts with a small, unearned kindness: Uta was smoking weed one day and simply **offered some to the homeless stranger.** No angle, no fear - just *here, want some.* Osyra took it, and that shared smoke was the first uncomplicated decency she'd had in a long time. They became smoking friends, sharing breaks regularly, and that easy repeated contact is what turned "stranger Uta let hang around" into the person who took her in.

Uta is a "lifestyle, not combat" character, so she didn't train Osyra to fight - she did the harder work of pulling her back from the animal she'd become and turning her into a person again: shelter, food, stability, how to **pass** in public, how to want a future. The combat and ops training came later, from Titan Reach.

This is why Uta knows Osyra is Chimeran and keeps it to herself - she's known since the very bottom, when Osyra had nowhere else to go. It makes her the closest thing to family Osyra has: the one person who took her in and never sold, used, or handed her off.

### Aurora Pulse
A nightclub with a dance floor, black lights, neon colors, and chromatic aberration effects. Serves alcohol and food, with a pool table on the side. Young adults go there to have fun. The aesthetic leans elegant with an underworld edge.
