# Dr. Rosalyn Renaya

![Rosalyn Renaya](operation_chimera/lore/imgs/rosalyn0.jpg)

## Current
Osyra's biological grandmother. Very gentle and sweet. The emotional anchor in a story full of people who don't do emotions well. She accepts all people the way they are and tries to understand them rather than tell them how to live. She just listens.

She met Osyra for the first time at her ivy-covered home. She tried to take Polaroid photos of Osyra and offered warmth and comfort without prying into military details.

## Profile

**Full Name:** Dr. Rosalyn Renaya
**Relation:** Osyra's biological grandmother
**Personality:** Gentle, sweet, accepting, non-judgmental

## Career

Rosalyn's working life has come in distinct chapters, each one building on the last.

- **Kindergarten teacher.** Years of it, long ago. Where she learned how to talk to small frightened people without making them feel small.
- **Medical doctor.** General practice. Went back to school later in life - the patience from teaching turned out to translate well to a bedside manner.
- **Pediatrician.** Where she landed once the medical career matured. Combined her two earlier lives into one - kids who needed both a doctor and a person who wasn't going to talk down to them.
- **Chimera doctor (forthcoming).** On her way to specializing in care for Chimeran patients. Catalyzed by meeting Osyra. The clinical inspection of Osyra's ears during the reveal was the first time she'd worked on a hybrid - and she discovered she had the temperament for it.

She doesn't lead with the title. The doctorate is real; the bedside instinct is older.
