# Lore

The main overarching story beyond the characters is that there is a lab that is creating a virus that turns humans into chimaran hybrids. We were trying to find the lab or the scientist and shut it down. But we are not very successful. It's well hidden. More come here and show up over time. We would do eventually find the head scientist through a bunch of quite illegal methods. These are the stories that got us there. From injection to revenge. along the way, Operative Osyra is tasked to deal with anomalous situations. Otherworldly creatures objects that warp reality. Things that bend the mind. Her only advantage is she's not actually human anymore. These entities sometimes do not always respond to her. And sometimes they respond to her specifically.

# Operation Chimera Characters

## Osyra
This character should appeal to people that are nihilistic, immediate action. Not very personable. Decisive military based. Treats everything like a military operation. Doesn't speak a significant amount until it's absolutely necessary. Some people get hurt or killed by the time this happens. She's darker brooding and more or less indifferent of other people. She saves them because it's her job, not because she wants to.
She is Chimeran, A human hybrid that came from a virus that was made in a lab. She was experimented on. The final result was a human that had wolf ears, wolf tail, and then elongated canines. Slightly increased strength, speed, balance, cunning instantly tactitional genius. Most obvious trait that this person is not human. Is they have red eyes. This appearance causes a commotion in civilian life, so she has adapted to hiding her traits. Using color contacts, hats, dresses, anything.

## Vanity
This is legitimately an Android powered by AI. This character should appeal to people that are interested in all data. This data will be collected morally, immorally, and any other method possible. This character does not acknowledge authority. This is Osyra's headquarters operative that keeps records of what Osyra is doing. This arrangement is acceptable because Osyra never writes or reports. She serves as headquarters journalist for the other operatives.
Vanity is rarely seen outside of headquarters, Because she is always taking notes on every operative. She is very fast to understand tactical situations and she is ridiculously strong. She is a closed network system meaning she cannot be hacked remotely. And the way she would interface with any digital object is she would physically have to lay her hands on it as a handshake. That's not to say she's highly observant in how things are connected.

## Uta
This character would appeal to female gang members. This character owns their own nightclub where Osyra often goes. Usa used to be the primary DJ of this nightclub and eventually was able to purchase the place off the owner. She still does DJ just because it's fun. Uta is into music tech.

## Layla
This character is mousey, meek, not very intimidating, very intelligent. This character assists Vanity With situations that require an emotional touch. Super empathetic.
This character has child syndrome. Always looks like a kid even though she's twenty. Her weak frame may have contributed to why she decided to understand digital world. In combat this character is mainly good at running and hiding. She is often found outsmarting her enemies versus fighting them head on.

## Korval
This character should appeal to Macho Men. A guy that served the US military. He is Russian. He has a Russian accent and has a tough personality. He enjoys his peace and quiet as much as possible. This guy almost has a sick sense about when things are not gonna go well. Even though he's a Osyra's bodyguard, he's often found cowering faster than she would. Especially against creatures of the other worlds.

## Rosalyn
This character should appeal to Christians. Not really Christian lady. This is Osyra's grandmother. Very gentle, sweet. She accepts all people the way they are and tries to understand them versus tell them how to live.
