# History

A chronological record of events, in order. **Dates are not yet fixed** - this chart tracks *sequence*, not calendar. Relative gaps ("two years later") are canon where stated; absolute dates are not.

**Ages** are Osyra's, and they are the spine: taken at **6**, sold to the lab at **16**, on the street until **18**, through Titan Reach from **18 to 20**, an Operator since, **29** in the present. A `~` means the age is inferred from the gaps around it, not stated on the page.

## The Chimera Project

Events at the lab, before Osyra.

| # | Event | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | The project begins as a **military** program | Engineer human-animal hybrids as a battlefield asset - predatory senses, instincts, reflexes. See [World Lore](operation_chimera/wiki/lore.md) |
| 2 | The virus is engineered in **numbered variants** | Chimera-9 is the wolf-human variant, the most stable, highest survival rate |
| 3 | **Teenagers are chosen on purpose** | Young enough for the body to adapt to the rewrite, old enough to fight |
| 4 | Most subjects **die or go feral** | Surviving the body is not keeping the mind. Some of what the Agency now hunts are feral Chimera-9 survivors |
| 5 | The military use proves **too unstable, too illegal** | The program stalls on its own results |
| 6 | Rebranded as **medicine** - the "cure" framing | The healing pitch is the alibi, not the point. See [The Chimera Vaccine](operation_chimera/wiki/con_vaccine.md) |
