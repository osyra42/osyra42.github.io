# Layla Null

![Layla Null](operation_chimera/wiki/images/layla0.jpg)

## Current
Mousey, meek, not intimidating, but extremely smart. The emotional support of the team—she assists Vanity with situations that require a human touch. Super empathetic.

She has a small, childlike frame despite being twenty. Her weak build may be why she chose to focus on the digital world rather than physical combat. In a fight, she's mainly good at running and hiding. She wins by outsmarting her enemies, not overpowering them.

She has not yet appeared in Revision 03.

## Profile

**Full Name:** Layla Null
**Hair:** Red
**Eyes:** Blue
**Build:** Small, childlike frame
**Age:** Twenty (appears much younger)

**Family Friend:** Nancy Mitts
**Role:** Vanity's handler/coworker at headquarters
**Digital Skills:** Programming, graphic design, 3D design, audio design, music

## Background

### Early Life
Adopted daughter of Alissa and Eric McKay, who are wealthy. Layla hasn't known struggle. Nancy Mitts is a family friend.

### Headquarters
At headquarters, Layla works alongside Vanity as her handler or coworker. Her digital skill set spans programming, graphic design, 3D design, audio design, and music—essentially everything you'd need to make a game, though she doesn't actually make games.

## History

### Revision 01
- Introduced as Layla McKay, daughter of Alissa and Eric McKay
- Designated as "Player 1" for Vanity
- Lost both parents in a car accident, taken in by Nancy Mitts
- Met Vanity for the first time in Chapter 11 and connected her to a laptop
- Featured across 22 chapters

### Revision 01 → 03
- Last name changed from McKay to Null. She is now the adopted daughter of the McKays rather than biological. She had no involvement in Vanity's creation. Nancy Mitts changed from caretaker after the parents' death to a family friend.
