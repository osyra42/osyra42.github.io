# The Agency

A chain-of-command organization that deals with anomalous entities, anomalous artifacts, and the situations the two leave behind. Operates quietly. Files everything.

The full name is not given on the page so far. Osyra refers to it as "the agency." Korval refers to it as "the agency." Vanity refers to it as "the agency."

## Mission

The agency exists to identify, contain, and clean up anomalies in the world - artifacts that warp reality, entities that don't follow normal rules, and the bleed-zones that form around them. Officially, much of the work is logged as cleanup of dangerous wildlife. Unofficially, that's about half the truth. The other half is retrieval of lost items for clients with money to make a problem go away, plus the work that doesn't get logged at all.

Recurring artifacts go on a **generational watch list** - anomalies that surface every few decades on a predictable cycle. The Book of Brine is on this list.

## Ranks

Five ranks, lowest to highest:

- **Janitor** - a separate team dispatched from Headquarters after the Operators are done. They clean up the site. When Janitors show up, the work is over.
- **Rookie** - Operator in training. Field-track. Not yet trusted with their own case.
- **Operator** - full field rank. Hunts anomalies, retrieves artifacts, handles incidents. Receives a callsign on assignment. Osyra is an Operator (callsign Chimera One).
- **Handler** - manages Operators from headquarters. Debriefs them, dispatches them, holds the file. Each Operator has a Handler. Some Handlers run more than one. Vanity is a Handler (nicknamed Wiki).
- **Director** - top of the agency. Not yet on the page.

## Operations

**Bodyguards.** Operators in the field are assigned a bodyguard. The bodyguard drives, flies, and stays present. They do not ask what's in the box. Korval is Osyra's bodyguard.

**Codenames.** Used during formal events - mission radio, official briefings, filed reports. Off-channel, people use real names. Osyra is "Chimera One" on comms; Osyra everywhere else. Vanity is "Wiki" on comms; Vanity everywhere else.

**Deployment.** Fast cases get private planes off the agency strip, cars waiting on the other end. The bodyguard flies with the Operator and drives on landing.

**Comms.** Operators carry a small pin clipped to the ear (designed to seat against the fur of a wolf ear without pulling, in Osyra's case). The pin warms when the channel is open. Transmissions open formally - *"Chimera One to Wiki, I have something to report."*

**Off the books.** Personal information picked up on an op can be flagged "off the record" inside a transmission. The Handler still logs it, just on a different shelf.

## Containment

**Containment boxes.** Black, lead-lined, with a switch on the lid. Adequate for most artifacts. Some artifacts continue to function through the lead - those need a deeper vault.

**Vaults.** Numbered. Vault four is the one referenced for higher-threat items.

**Containment desk.** Sits in the lounge between the vault corridor and the rest of headquarters. A desk officer weighs, scans, and logs incoming items, then passes them through the slot.

**Cleanup.** Janitors finish the site after Operators leave. By the time they arrive, headquarters has decided the case is over.

## Headquarters

A working building. Long tables, padded chairs, coffee that's always on. Low-light displays line the lounge wall, showing current vault inventory color-coded by threat tier. An armory with a senior rack where senior Operators' field kits are pre-staged. A landing strip for the private plane. A ficus in the lounge that has been alive a long time.
