# Dr. Rosalyn Renaya

![Rosalyn Renaya](operation_chimera/wiki/imgs/rosalyn0.jpg)

## Current
Osyra's biological grandmother. Very gentle and sweet. The emotional anchor in a story full of people who don't do emotions well. She accepts all people the way they are and tries to understand them rather than tell them how to live. She just listens.

Holds a doctorate (specifics TBD — likely medical, given how clinically she inspected Osyra's ears during the reveal scene). She doesn't lead with the title.

She met Osyra for the first time at her ivy-covered home. She tried to take Polaroid photos of Osyra and offered warmth and comfort without prying into military details.
