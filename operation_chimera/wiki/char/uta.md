# Uta Yuki

![Uta Yuki](operation_chimera/wiki/imgs/uta0.jpg)

## Current
She owns a nightclub called **Aurora Pulse** that Osyra often hangs out at. She used to be the primary DJ there and eventually bought the place from the previous owner. She still DJs just because it's fun. More of a lifestyle character than a combat one. Into music and tech.

She has not yet appeared in Revision 03.

### Aurora Pulse
A nightclub with a dance floor, black lights, neon colors, and chromatic aberration effects. Serves alcohol and food, with a pool table on the side. Young adults go there to have fun. The aesthetic leans elegant with an underworld edge.

## History

### Revision 01
- Appeared in Chapter 07 through social media showing her transformation
- Ran a mocktail bar and all-ages venue called "The Bassment"
- Previously saved by Osyra during a violent cue ball incident at a club
- Created a signature mocktail called the "Shattered Cue Ball" to own her traumatic experience
- Carried a .380 caliber pistol as her own bouncer
- Offered Osyra a lifetime of free drinks

### Revision 01 → 03
- Her backstory from Revision 01 has not yet been referenced in the current revision.
