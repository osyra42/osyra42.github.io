## Background

### The facility
Eric and Alissa McKay own a facility of automation that designs and builds androids and automated machines. Their work splits into two directions:
- **Vanity** - an advanced, conscious AI android built as a **prototype**. She was never put into production; the model proved too expensive to reproduce, so the line stopped at her (see [Vanity](operation_chimera/wiki/chr_vanity.md)).
- **The combat line** - cheaper, heavier-metal units built for combat, with onboard weapons, closer to mech suits. They are piloted (driven around) or set up on simple automated patrols.

### Family
The McKays are wealthy. Their adopted daughter, Layla Null, works at headquarters as Vanity's handler/coworker (see [Layla Null](operation_chimera/wiki/chr_layla.md)).
